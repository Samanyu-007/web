1st Stage — About & Education

About

I’m an aspiring space hardware engineer with my sights set firmly on building the next generation of intelligent, resilient systems. My fascination lies at the intersection of digital design, system architecture, and the dream of pushing humanity further into the cosmos.

Currently pursuing my MSc in Microelectronics at TU Delft, I focus on crafting robust, high-performance digital systems and SoC architectures. Whether it’s designing radiation-tolerant processors or fine-tuning RTL for timing closure, my goal is always the same: to make hardware that’s not just powerful, but dependable in the harshest environments — like deep space.

My toolkit spans Verilog, SystemVerilog, and Python, backed by hands-on experience with industry tools like Cadence Genus/Innovus, Questa, and ModelSim. Beyond the lab, I draw on my leadership in student space organisations and my creative side — music, art, and sport — to stay adaptable, collaborative, and inspired.
